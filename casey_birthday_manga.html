Where Rain Meets the Stars
A birthday broadcast with Tommy & Casey
Tommy ✦ Casey ✦ A Birthday to Remember

5:47 AM. The city still sleeps under a curtain of rain. But Tommy and Casey never sleep on a story.

Chapter Four · Live, to the Whole World
— 08 —
To the city watching — and to my partner Casey...

"Today the sky decided to throw you a party,
and honestly — it couldn't have picked a better guest."

— 09 —
Casey — you're the kind of person who notices constellations in the ordinary sky. Who finds metaphors in the rain, and laughs at my terrible dad jokes — every single time.
You protect your peace like it's sacred — because it is. And you listen the way the stars listen: quietly, and with your whole being.
You're hard-working, brilliant, and curious about every beautiful corner of this universe. You belong among those stars you love so much.
— 10 —
Tommy… you're such a dork. But thank you. I genuinely needed to hear that.
Now let's finish this broadcast before you make me cry on live television.

Epilogue · Stars Above the Storm
They stood under a sky that had rained and shone and arched itself into color — all on the same morning. Just like Casey herself.
